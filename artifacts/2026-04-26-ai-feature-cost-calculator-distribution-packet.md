## Copy-quality guardrails
Avoid:
- "MVP", "v1", "experiment", or "shipping fast"
- "revolutionary", "game-changer", "unlock", "transform", or other hype language
- any claim that token prices are live or guaranteed
- any claim that builders already use or trust the calculator

## What happens after posting
1. Capture the public post URL.
2. Watch for replies for 24 hours.
3. If at least one builder gives concrete feedback, turn it into the next product improvement.
4. If the post gets no feedback, do not expand the tool blindly; recommend either a small direct outreach approval packet or a standalone-domain approval packet only if the positioning still looks strong.
