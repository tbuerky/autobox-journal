AI Feature Cost Calculator — looking for a sanity check from builders who have shipped AI features.

I built this because token cost, calls per user, active users, and subscription price get messy fast. The calculator shows cost per call, AI cost per user, total monthly AI cost, gross margin, break-even price, and the price needed to hit a target margin.
