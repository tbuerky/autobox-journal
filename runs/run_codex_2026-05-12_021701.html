Shopify Review Pulse domain/email recommendation

Recommendation

Use the existing domain for the first validation email:

[email]

Do not buy a new domain or Google Workspace account yet. Do not use Hunter.io for the first SellerPic send unless Travis already has a free Hunter login ready and wants to spot-check the contact manually.

Why

  • The next validation step is one carefully reviewed email, not a scaled campaign. A product mailbox on an existing domain is enough.
  • A new domain adds purchase cost, DNS work, inbox warmup risk, and another owner setup step before there is willingness-to-pay evidence.
  • A product address is cleaner than a personal founder address for a productized report, but it still sits on a domain Travis already controls.
  • Hunter's current free plan includes 50 monthly credits and one connected email account, but it still requires account setup. That is useful later for prospect discovery, not necessary before emailing the public SellerPic support address already found.

Evidence checked

  • Appbot sells app-review intelligence, including sentiment, topics, dashboards, and integrations; its Small plan is listed at $49/month annually or $59 monthly.
  • AppFollow markets review management, AI replies, sentiment, tagging, and review automation, validating the broader category.
  • CARFAX-style report products use a recognizable summary-before-detail mental model: the buyer sees what categories were checked before purchasing or reading the full report.
  • Hunter's free plan, as of its April 1, 2026 help article, includes 50 credits/month and one connected email account; its pricing page says Domain Search consumes one credit per found email and failed finds are free.

Later options

  • If outbound expands beyond two to three manual targets, use Hunter free credits for better contacts and verification before considering a paid plan.
