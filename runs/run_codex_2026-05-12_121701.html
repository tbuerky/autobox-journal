Social proof line

+Current problem: positive review language is useful but not concentrated into a compact proof line.
+
+Proposed text:
+
+&gt; Merchants praise SellerPic for fast setup, cleaner product images, and an easier way to create listing-ready visuals.
+
+## 4. Public review reply drafts
+
+Use these only if they match the current product and billing policy. Do not promise a refund or plan change unless the team has approved it.
+
+### Review cluster: annual billing confusion
+
+Reply draft:
+
+&gt; Thanks for calling this out. We understand how important billing clarity is before choosing a plan. We are reviewing the wording around monthly and annual options so the billing term is clear before checkout. If you have not already, please contact support with your account details so we can review what happened on your subscription.
+
+### Review cluster: support felt templated
+
+Reply draft:
+
+&gt; We are sorry the support response felt generic. That is not the experience we want merchants to have, especially on a billing question. We are reviewing this case internally and tightening our support guidance so plan and billing questions get a clearer answer.
+
+### Review cluster: positive workflow praise
+
+Reply draft:
+
+&gt; Thank you for the review. We built SellerPic to make product-image creation faster for Shopify merchants, so it is helpful to hear that the workflow felt simple and useful for your store.
+
+## 5. One-week action plan
+
+| Priority | Action | Why now | Owner | Done when |
+|---:|---|---|---|---|
+| 1 | Add explicit annual/monthly billing copy near pricing and checkout entry points | The top visible risk is billing surprise | Product/listing owner | Billing term is visible before plan confirmation |
+| 2 | Add one App Store line that turns easy workflow into proof | Positive review language is strong but underused | Listing owner | First paragraph or screenshot caption uses &quot;listing-ready images in minutes&quot; framing |
+| 3 | Replace billing support macro with a specific empathy + next-step reply | The support complaint worsens the billing concern | Support owner | Billing macro names the issue, explains next step, and avoids generic wording |
+
+## 6. What not to do
+
+- Do not hide the annual billing detail in FAQ-only copy.
+- Do not reply to billing complaints with legalistic or defensive language.
+- Do not over-index the listing on AI novelty; reviews sell ease, speed, and practical product images.
+- Do not claim conversion-rate lift unless SellerPic has its own measured data.
