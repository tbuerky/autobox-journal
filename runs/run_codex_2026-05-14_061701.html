Shopify Review Pulse Paid Report Scaffold Generator

Outcome

Created a private paid-report scaffold generator for Shopify Review Pulse:

  • build_paid_report_scaffold.mjs
  • build_paid_report_scaffold.test.mjs
  • sellerpic-paid-report-scaffold.md

The generator turns an existing public Review Pulse prospect report into a paid-report scaffold with prefilled metadata, install-driver rows, trust-risk rows, and compliance reminders. It is meant to reduce fulfillment time after payment or explicit sample approval, not to create a new public teaser.

Why This Was The Right Single Task

All immediate external revenue motions are still owner-gated:

1. Send the SellerPic test from an accessible owner mailbox.

2. Verify the branded Google mailbox.

3. Create the free Tally intake form.

4. Create the Stripe Payment Link.

5. Publish the founder post.

The useful non-gated move was to make paid fulfillment faster and more repeatable. This supports the lane's actual revenue path: if SellerPic or a fallback prospect replies, the $49 report can be produced from a generated prospect report with less manual setup and fewer policy mistakes.

+
+Source: https://apps.shopify.com/example/reviews
+Generated: 2026-05-14
+
+## Snapshot
+
+- Public rating: 4.7/5 across 120 reviews.
+- Review text items sampled from public review pages: 98.
+- Strongest positive theme: Workflow / ease of use.
+- Strongest risk theme: Support experience.
+
+## What buyers already believe
+
+- Workflow / ease of use: 18 positive hit(s), 1 risk hit(s). Example: &quot;Easy setup and fast results.&quot;
+- Support experience: 11 positive hit(s), 2 risk hit(s). Example: &quot;Support helped us fix the widget.&quot;
+
+## What can reduce installs
+
+- Support experience: 2 risk hit(s). Example: &quot;Support took too long to answer.&quot;
+- Pricing / billing trust: 1 risk hit(s). Example: &quot;The annual plan was not clear.&quot;
+`;

+test(&#x27;parses a generated Review Pulse prospect report&#x27;, () =&gt; {
+  const report = parseReviewPulseReport(fixture);
+
+  assert.equal(report.appName, &#x27;Example Reviews App&#x27;);
+  assert.equal(report.source, &#x27;https://apps.shopify.com/example/reviews&#x27;);
+  assert.equal(report.publicRating, &#x27;4.7/5&#x27;);
+  assert.equal(report.publicReviewCount, &#x27;120&#x27;);
+  assert.equal(report.sampledReviewCount, &#x27;98&#x27;);
+  assert.equal(report.positiveThemes.length, 2);
+  assert.equal(report.riskThemes.length, 2);
